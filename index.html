Privacy Policy

Fantazy Arena — Instagram @fntzyarena · YouTube @FntzyArena

Last updated: 13 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Fantazy Arena ("we", "us") and the private automation tool "Hermes FntzyArena" handle data when used to manage our own social media accounts on Instagram (@fntzyarena) and YouTube (@FntzyArena).

1. Who this applies to

The "Hermes FntzyArena" application is a private, single-operator tool used solely by the owner of the Fantazy Arena accounts to manage those accounts. It is not offered to, installed by, or made available to any third party, and it does not onboard other users.

2. What data we access

  • Our own account content: media we publish (Reels, Stories, photos, videos), their captions, and engagement metrics on our own posts.
  • Public comments on our own posts and their author usernames, so we can read and reply to our audience.
  • We do not access private messages, contacts, or data belonging to people outside of interactions they choose to have on our public posts.

3. How we use it

Data is used only to publish our own content and to read and respond to comments on our own posts. We do not sell, rent, share, or transfer any data to third parties, and we do not use it for advertising or profiling.

4. Where data is stored

Access tokens and operational data are stored locally on the operator's own computer and are not hosted on any public server. We retain only what is needed to operate the accounts and delete it when no longer needed.

5. Third-party platforms

Our use of the Instagram and YouTube platforms is subject to their respective terms and privacy policies (Meta Platforms, Inc. and Google LLC). Platform data is handled in accordance with the Meta Platform Terms and Developer Policies.

6. Data deletion

Because this tool only manages our own accounts and does not store other people's personal data, there is no third-party user data to delete. Any account holder who has commented on our posts may delete their own comments at any time directly on the platform. For any request related to this policy, contact us below.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy: admin@fantazyarena.com

We may update this policy; the "Last updated" date above reflects the latest revision.
